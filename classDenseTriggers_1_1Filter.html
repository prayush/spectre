Detailed Description

Filter activations of a dense trigger using a non-dense trigger.

For example, to trigger every 10 starting at 100, one could use

Filter:
Trigger:
Times:
EvenlySpaced:
Offset: 0
Interval: 10
Filter:
TimeCompares:
Comparison: GreaterThanOrEqualTo
Value: 100
