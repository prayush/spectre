      37             :  * \f$r > r_{\rm out}\f$. In the region \f$ r_{\rm in} < r < r_{\rm out}\f$,
      38             :  * the solution transitions such that the logarithms of the density and
      39             :  * pressure vary linearly. E.g., if \f$\rho(r < r_{\rm in}) = \rho_{\rm in}\f$

      45             :  * \f]

      49             :  * antiparallel to the z axis. In the transverse (e.g., x and y) dimensions, the

      51             :  * at the final time. E.g., if `InnerRadius = 0.8`, `OuterRadius = 1.0`, and
      52             :  * the final time is 4.0, a good domain extends from `(x,y)=(-6.0, -6.0)` to
